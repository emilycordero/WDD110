Welcome to Heavenly Fresh, where we believe that healthy eating should be easy and delicious. Our menu features a variety of healthy shakes, refreshers, and teas that are made with fresh, natural ingredients.

Shakes: - Berry Blast: A refreshing blend of mixed berries, banana, almond milk, and honey. - Peanut Butter Cup: A delicious combination of chocolate protein powder, peanut butter, banana, and almond milk. - Green Machine: A nutrient-packed shake made with spinach, avocado, banana, pineapple, and coconut water. - Mango Tango: A tropical delight made with mango, banana, coconut milk, and honey. - Vanilla Almond: A classic shake made with vanilla protein powder, almond milk, banana, and honey. Refreshers: - Cucumber Mint: A refreshing drink made with cucumber, mint, lime, and honey. - Ginger Turmeric: A spicy and healthy drink made with ginger, turmeric, lemon, and honey. - Pineapple Ginger: A tropical twist on the classic ginger drink, made with pineapple, ginger, and honey. - Watermelon Lime: A sweet and tangy drink made with watermelon, lime, and honey. Teas: - Matcha Green Tea: A traditional Japanese tea made with high-quality matcha powder and almond milk. - Hibiscus Tea: A refreshing and tart tea made with hibiscus flowers, lemon, and honey. - Chamomile Lavender: A calming and soothing tea made with chamomile flowers, lavender, and honey. - Peppermint Tea: A refreshing and invigorating tea made with peppermint leaves and honey. All of our shakes, refreshers, and teas are made with fresh, natural ingredients and are free of artificial flavors, colors, and sweeteners. We also offer vegan and gluten-free options to accommodate all dietary needs. Come visit us and discover the delicious and healthy world of Heavenly Fresh.
