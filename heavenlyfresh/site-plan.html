Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help get more clients for Heavenly Fresh LLC. Thus I must make an appealing website that entices clients and demonstrates character of the business owner and their menu. It'll have some pictures of the events they hold and a menu of their products as well as their prices. In the contact us page they can make orders and leave a call back number for when their order is ready.

Audience

18-40 year olds who want to have a healthier lifestyle and wants alternative healthy refreshers, shakes, and coffee.
